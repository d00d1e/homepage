Discover Sushi

Omakaze serves the carefully crafted menu of Chef Akira Yoshizumi. With ingredients sourced both domestically and internationally, he crafts a very special tasting menu rooted his own style of Edomae sushi, learned from his sushi master from Tokyo. Chef Yoshizumi is a strong believer in making sure the food he serves represents the waters he is surrounded by. Only the freshest and best ingredients, prepared with dedication, love, and passion find their way to your plate.

Our Mission
